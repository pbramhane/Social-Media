Account Management

  • Account Settings: Manage your account settings, including privacy, security, notifications, linked accounts, and more, through the settings menu in your profile.


    Posting Content:


  • Uploading Photos and Videos: Learn how to upload and share photos and videos from your device to your profile or stories.

Interacting with Content:


  • Likes and Comments: Understand how to like, comment, and engage with posts from other users.

Discovering Content:


  • Exploring and Searching: Explore content on Instagram by using the search function, explore tab, and hashtags to find accounts and posts of interest.
  • Following Accounts: Learn how to follow accounts to see their posts in your feed and keep up with their updates.

Privacy and Safety:


  • Profile Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings to control who can view your content, send you messages, or tag you in posts.
  • Reporting and Blocking: Report inappropriate content or accounts and block users to ensure a safer and more comfortable experience on the platform.
